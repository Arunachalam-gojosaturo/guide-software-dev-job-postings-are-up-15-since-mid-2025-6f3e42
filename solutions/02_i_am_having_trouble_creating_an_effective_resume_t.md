# I am having trouble creating an effective resume to showcase my programming skills to potential employers

_Craft a compelling resume by highlighting relevant projects and experiences_

## Steps

1. Use a template from a website like Canva or Resume.io to create a visually appealing resume
2. List your most relevant programming projects, including a brief description and technologies used
3. Use the terminal command `git log --all --decorate --oneline --graph` to generate a log of your GitHub contributions
4. Include any relevant certifications or online courses completed, such as those on Coursera or Udemy
5. Use language from the job posting to describe your skills and experiences, and tailor your resume to each job application

## Pitfalls

- Including irrelevant information
- Not tailoring your resume to each job application
