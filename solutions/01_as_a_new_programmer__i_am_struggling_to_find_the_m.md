# As a new programmer, I am struggling to find the most in-demand programming skills to focus on for a successful career

_Identify the most sought-after programming skills by analyzing job postings on popular platforms_

## Steps

1. Visit the GitHub Jobs portal at https://github.com/jobs and filter by 'programming language' to see the most in-demand skills
2. Use the terminal command `curl https://api.github.com/jobs | grep 'programming language'` to fetch and parse job postings
3. Analyze the results using tools like Excel or Google Sheets to identify trends and patterns
4. Create a list of the top 5 most in-demand programming skills and research online courses or tutorials to learn them
5. Join online communities like Reddit's r/learnprogramming to network with other programmers and stay updated on industry trends

## Pitfalls

- Not considering the job market in your location
- Focusing too much on a single skill
